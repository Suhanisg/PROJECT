Crispy Pork Chops

Holiday Bites & Delights Series

Crispy Pork Chops:

Incredibly flavorful, undeniably juicy pork chops sheathed in a crackly, parmesan-herb crust. Pan fried until golden, and then baked to crispy perfection, these pork chops are completely foolproof, and they make the ultimate quick-and-easy weeknight meal.

Recipe:

Ingredients For Crispy Pork Chops:

  • 4 boneless pork chops (about 1 inch thick)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups breadcrumbs (preferably panko for extra crispiness)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (optional, for extra richness).

Step-by-Step Guide For Crispy Pork Chops:

Step 1: Pat the Pork Chops Dry Pat the pork chops dry with paper towels. This helps the coating stick better and ensures crispiness.

Step 2: Season the Pork Chops Season both sides of the pork chops generously with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.

Step 3: Set Up the Dredging Station In a shallow bowl, place the flour. In another shallow bowl, beat the eggs. In a third bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with Parmesan cheese (if using) and a pinch of salt and pepper. Step 4: Coat the Pork Chops First, dredge each pork chop in the flour, ensuring it's evenly coated. Dip it into the beaten eggs. Finally, coat it in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs stick well.

Step 5: Fry the Pork Chops Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the pork chops to the pan (you may need to fry them in batches depending on the size of your skillet). Cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the pork chops are golden brown and crispy. For extra richness, you can add butter to the pan in the last 2 minutes of frying for a buttery finish.

Step 6: Rest the Pork Chops Once the pork chops are crispy and cooked through, remove them from the pan and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb any excess oil.

Step 7: Serve Let the pork chops rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh salad.

Enjoy your crispy, juicy pork chops!
